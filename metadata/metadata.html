Dapper Doggos


Dogs in tophats are just about the cutest things ever. Check out some fun facts about these cute little friends.

Top hats can add class to any type of outfit, but have you ever added it to your pet's daily wear? Adding a top hat makes your dog look sooo handsome. Some of the cutest dogs in top hats are corgis and pugs, but a top hat makes anyone look good

Would you ever put your dog in a top hat? Maybe for special events or holidays you can add a top hat to make them sparkle. But always remember if your dog doesn't like it, do not force him to wear it
